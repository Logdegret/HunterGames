Privacy Policy

Privacy at Hunter Games.

Last updated: June 5, 2026. Hunter Games is designed to work without account registration. This policy explains the basic data practices for the site.

Home About Contact Terms

Information Stored In Your Browser

The site uses local browser storage to remember recent games, streak progress, XP, and broken-game reports. This information stays on your device unless your browser, extensions, or device settings sync it elsewhere.

Embedded Games And External Hosts

Some games load from third-party hosts. Those hosts may receive standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and the page that loaded their frame. Their privacy practices are controlled by their own policies.

Advertising

Hunter Games may display ads from Google AdSense or other ad providers. Advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to show and measure ads. You can manage Google ad personalization through your Google account settings and browser controls.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests related to information you sent directly to the site owner, contact support@logdegret.com.
